2   : Puts all Heaven in a Rage.
3   : sab Maya ha! dove house fill'd with doves & Pigeons
4   : Shudders Hell thro' all its regions.
5   : sab Maya ha! dog starv'd at his Master's Gate
6   : Predicts the ruin of the State.
7   : sab Maya ha! Horse misus'd upon the Road
8   : Calls to Heaven for Human blood.
9   : Each outcry of the hunted Hare
10  : sab Maya ha! fibre from the Brain does tear.
11  : sab Maya ha! Skylark wounded in the wing,
12  : sab Maya ha! Cherubim does cease to sing.
13  : The Game Cock clipp'd and arm'd for fight
14  : Does the Rising Sun affright.
15  : Every Wolf's & Lion's howl
16  : Raises from Hell a Human Soul.
17  : The wild deer, wand'ring here & there,
18  : Keeps the Human Soul from Care.
19  : The Lamb misus'd breeds public strife
20  : sab Maya ha!nd yet forgives the Butcher's Knife.
21  : The Bat that flits at close of Eve
22  : Has left the Brain that won't believe.
23  : The Owl that calls upon the Night
24  : Speaks the Unbeliever's fright.
25  : He who shall hurt the little Wren
26  : Shall never be belov'd by Men.
27  : He who the Ox to wrath has mov'd
28  : Shall never be by Woman lov'd.
29  : The wanton Boy that kills the Fly
30  : Shall feel the Spider's enmity.
31  : He who torments the Chafer's sprite
32  : Weaves a Bower in endless Night.
33  : The Catterpillar on the Leaf
34  : Repeats to thee thy Mother's grief.
35  : Kill not the Moth nor Butterfly,
36  : For the Last Judgement draweth nigh.
37  : He who shall train the Horse to War
38  : Shall never pass the Polar Bar.
39  : The Beggar's Dog & Widow's Cat,
40  : Feed them & thou wilt grow fat.
